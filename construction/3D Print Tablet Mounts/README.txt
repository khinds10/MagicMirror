# Summary

I have previously made a few wall mount for just about everything in my house. However, my printer is not big enough for me to print a mount for my old samsung galaxy tab 10 in the same style as the other mount. 

Therefore this mount is slightly different. It consist of two corner brackets, and you can then align the brackets to the distance that fits the size of you tablet. And you can choose whether you want it upright or level. Nice and flexible :)
